How do affiliate links work?

When you click an affiliate link on our site and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. We only recommend products and services we genuinely believe add value. Every affiliate relationship is disclosed in accordance with FTC guidelines.

I have an issue with a product I purchased through a link

Since we're an affiliate marketer, we don't sell products directly. Please contact the merchant's customer support team for order issues, returns, or refunds. If you're unsure who to contact, email us and we'll point you in the right direction.

Do you offer refunds for your services?

Our consulting and strategy services are custom-tailored to each client. Payment terms and cancellation policies are outlined in your service agreement. For questions, reach out to hello@intentcollective.com.

How can I work with Intent Collective?

We'd love to hear from you! Visit our contact page or email us directly to discuss your brand's goals. We work with businesses of all sizes on affiliate strategy, content creation, and digital growth.
